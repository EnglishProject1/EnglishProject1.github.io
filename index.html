The Story

The Chernobyl nuclear accident happened [insert age here] years ago, and it caused the contamination of the area Because of this there were many deaths. It was necessary to evacuate 116 thousands of people within the radius of 30 kilometres. It is considered the worst accident in the world along with Fukushima (2011). The cause of this explosion was due to an error on the 4th reactor, during a safety test. This test was postponed by 10 hours from the original time. Because of this, the original team who was prepared to take the test, was been replaced with another team. The toxic cloud extended on a very large area of europe causing a lot of worries about the contamination of vegetables and plants. The disaster of Chernobyl released a large amount of radiation: at least 100 times more than the atomic bombes of Hiroshima and Nagasaki. In fact a forest nearby became brown before dying. It is one of the most radioactives places in the world in fact it is called red forest.
